Can you choose the right element to defeat your virtual opponent?

Rules

  • Select a button to choose what element to play
  • The computer will choose an element at random
  • Fire defeats grass
  • Grass defeats water
  • Water defeats fire
  • If your chosen element defeats your opponent, you gain a point
  • If your chosen element is defeated by your opponent, they gain a point
  • If you choose the same element as your opponent, no points are awarded
  • The first player to gain five points wins the entire game
